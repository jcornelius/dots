Dots

The Official Side-Game of Sticks & Steaks

1 Dot
Skin
Win the hole outright — no ties.
Closest to the Pin
Nearest to the hole on a par 3 after all players have teed off.
Greenie
Land on the green from the tee shot on a par 3.
Barky
Make par on a hole after your ball has hit a tree.
Sandy
Make par after your ball has been in a bunker.
Poley
Drain a putt longer than the flagpole.
2 Dots
Chippy
Hole out from off the green — chip, pitch, or bump-and-run.
Birdie
Complete the hole in one under par.
Wet Dream
Save par after your ball has been in a water hazard. Skipping it off the water still counts.
Low Putts (per 9)
The player with the fewest puts on each 9 holes.
Low Gross Score (per 9)
Total strokes scored.
Low Net Score (per 9)
Handicap adjusted strokes scored.
3 Dots
Eagle
Complete the hole in two under par.
4 Dots
Ace
Hole in one. Buy a round.
Albatross
Complete the hole in three under par.

Mulligans

  • Each player is allowed 6 mulligans per side. Taking a mulligan costs 1 dot, and disqualifies you for score dots, but you remain eligible for stroke dots.
    Example: you can still win a 'Barky' or 'Sandy' but not a Skin, Eagle, etc.
